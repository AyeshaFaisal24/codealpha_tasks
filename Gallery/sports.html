Artist Spotlight: Derrick Knox

“As a former athlete turned artist, I see motion differently. Every burst of speed, every dive, every fall—it’s choreography. It’s rhythm. I try to capture that pulse in every brushstroke.”

Derrick’s art brings movement to stillness, using dynamic textures and bold color clashes to mimic the adrenaline of sports. From street basketball courts to Olympic stadiums, his murals bring athleticism to life through layered energy.
