Artist Spotlight: Maya Cortez

"Street art is the voice of the voiceless, the color of the colorless, and the story of the untold. My work seeks to bridge the gap between the marginalized and the mainstream, creating conversations where there were once only walls."

Maya's work has transformed urban landscapes across 14 countries, blending traditional techniques with modern street art to create powerful social commentary.
