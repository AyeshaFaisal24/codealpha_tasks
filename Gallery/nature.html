Artist Spotlight: Norah Feng

“I sit with the trees. I wait for the wind. I walk barefoot when I can. My art begins when I stop trying to control it.”

Norah’s work blends traditional brush techniques with natural materials—pressed flowers, handmade earth pigments, and recycled paper—to create art that literally grows from nature.
